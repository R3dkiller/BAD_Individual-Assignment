Italian Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup pasta
  • 1 cup cannellini beans
  • 1 cup diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup carrots, diced
  • 1/2 cup celery, diced
  • 1/2 cup onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 4 cups vegetable broth
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add garlic, onions, carrots, and celery. Saut√© until vegetables are tender.
  2. Add diced tomatoes, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Cook for a few minutes.
  3. Stir in vegetable broth, cannellini beans, and pasta. Simmer until pasta is cooked al dente.
  4. Adjust seasoning to taste. If the soup is too thick, add more vegetable broth.
  5. Serve hot, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese.
