Lasagna

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 ounces each) tomato sauce
  • 2 cans (6 ounces each) tomato paste
  • 2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) diced tomatoes, undrained
  • 2 teaspoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups ricotta or cottage cheese
  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 9 cooked lasagna noodles, drained

Instructions:

  1. In a large skillet, cook ground beef, onion, and garlic over medium heat until meat is no longer pink. Drain excess fat.
  2. Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, diced tomatoes, sugar, basil, and salt. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. In a bowl, combine ricotta or cottage cheese with beaten eggs.
  4. Layer ingredients in a 9x13-inch baking dish as follows: 1/4 of the sauce, 3 lasagna noodles, 1/3 of the ricotta mixture, 1 cup mozzarella, and 1/4 cup Parmesan. Repeat for two more layers, ending with remaining sauce, mozzarella, and Parmesan.
  5. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-35 minutes or until bubbly. Let stand 10 minutes before serving.
