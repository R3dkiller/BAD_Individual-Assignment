Italian Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked pasta
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup black olives, sliced
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tablespoons fresh basil, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinaigrette
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the pasta according to package instructions, then let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine pasta, cherry tomatoes, black olives, red onion, feta cheese, and fresh basil.
  3. Drizzle with balsamic vinaigrette and toss gently to combine.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 30 minutes before serving.
