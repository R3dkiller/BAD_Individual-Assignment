Italian Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound pasta (spaghetti or your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves for garnish
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using), saut√© until fragrant.
  3. Stir in crushed tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.
  4. Simmer the sauce for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  6. Garnish with fresh basil leaves and serve with grated Parmesan cheese.
